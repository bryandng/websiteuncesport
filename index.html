UNC ESPORTS

We are a video gaming community that welcomes video game players from all walks of life. Whether you are a hardcore competitive gamer or you are more into casual games, there is a place for you with us. We strive to achieve camaraderie in the eSports community not only to our campus at UNCCH, but to all schools around us.
